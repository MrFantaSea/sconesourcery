✦ The bakehouse

One baker, five recipes, old-country roots.

Scones are Scottish — so it’s a little on-brand that I’m mostly Nordic with a good pour of Scottish, Irish, and a splash of northern German in me. Close enough that butter and cold hands feel like an inheritance. It’s one pair of hands in South Jersey and five planned scones I won’t stop fussing over; the approved operating kitchen is still to be finalized.

I fell for scones young — the smell filling the whole house, everybody drifting toward the oven like it was pulling them in. That pull is the only sourcery I deal in: the warm hush right before something good comes out. The star on the seal is for that. I’d like to hand a little of it to you.
