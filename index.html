✦ One baker behind the door

The only trick is caring too much.

I fell for scones young—the smell filling the house, everyone drifting toward the oven as if it were pulling them in.

That warm hush right before something good comes out is the whole idea behind Scone Sourcery. For now it is one pair of hands in South Jersey, five recipes under development, and an operating kitchen still to be finalized.

“The sourcery is real. The sorcery is mostly butter.”
